## Positioning statement

**لارا للجمال** is a Kuwaiti wellness brand that curates **premium gummy supplements** for sleep, energy, and focus — sold with the care and presentation of a local boutique, not a generic dropship catalog. We speak to **busy Kuwaiti men and women** who want results they can feel, with **clear ingredients, honest benefits, and COD convenience**.

## Why we can charge premium

1. **Branded unboxing story** (copy + visuals even if dropshipped)
2. **Ingredient transparency** — names, doses where available, "why it works"
3. **Authority stack** — quality standards, lab/testing *claims only if true* (use "مصنع وفق معايير الجودة" unless certs provided)
4. **Social proof** — reviews, UGC-style quotes, order counters (real once live)
5. **Bundle economics** — 3-pack at 29 KWD feels like smart buying vs 16×3=48

## Competitive differentiation (vs random TikTok shops)

- Consistent **Lara Beauty** visual system (not AliExpress template)
- **Arabic-first** UX, KWD, Kuwait phone, COD
- **Single-brand catalog** (3 heroes — depth not width)
- **Educational sections** on each PDP (how to use, who it's for, timeline)

## Voice rules

- Use **أنتِ/أنت** sparingly; prefer **"جسمك"، "يومك"، "نومك"**
- Avoid exaggerated medical claims: no "يعالج الأرق المزمن" → use "يساعد على تحسين جودة النوم"
- Always pair benefit with **mechanism** or **ingredient**
- Include **Kuwait COD** reassurance: "ادفع عند الاستلام — بدون مخاطرة"

## English usage

- Header: Arabic primary, **Lara Beauty** subline in English
- Footer legal can be AR + short EN
- Meta/tracking in EN OK; UI copy AR
